What is volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport in which two teams of six people are seperated by a net.
Each team tries to score points only minimum of three hits and the ball goes in the other teams side,
but if they don't hit the ball, the points goes on the other team.

Is volleyball popular in New Zealand?

Volleyball has become very very popular in New Zealand and in secondary schools.
Almost 17,000 kids represented their schools in volleyball in 2016.
Volleyball has become very popular in secondary schools.

Basic rule in volleyball

1.Only 6 players on the courts at all times.:3 in the front row and 3 in the back row.
2.There is maximum of 3 hits in the game per side.
3.points are made on every serve for the winning team for their rally.
4.The first team reach up to 25 points and by at least two points wins a set.
